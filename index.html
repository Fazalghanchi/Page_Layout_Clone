Status

The FreeBSd/ppc port still a Tier 2 platform. that means It is not being fully supported by our security
officer , release engineers and toolchain maintainers

Latest News

  • 03 April, 2009 : Support For the power pc G5 has been committed to SVN
  • 03 March , 2008 : Support For Freescale PowerQUICC III MPC85XXX family system-on-chip
    development boards has been committed into CVS
  • 27 February , 2008 : FreeBSD 7.0 is the first release to officially support the FreeBSD/ppc Port.
